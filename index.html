ProsodyBERT: Self-Supervised Prosody Representation for Controllable-TTS

Abstract

This work proposes ProsodyBERT, a self-supervised approach to learning prosody representations from raw audio via HuBERT-like masked unit prediction. Experiments show that the TTS model trained with ProsodyBERT features can generate natural and expressive speech, outperforming the model trained with energy and pitch. Also, the style and expressiveness of synthesized audio can be controlled by manipulating the prosody features.
